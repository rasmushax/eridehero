Our mission

Making sense of the electric ride market

ERideHero started in 2019 when Rasmus Barslund noticed a problem: the micro-mobility market was growing fast, but there was nowhere to get unbiased information.

Since then, we've helped 8 million+ readers and 800K+ YouTube viewers find the right ride. We publish in-depth reviews and buying guides, and we've built tools for price tracking, deal alerts, product comparisons, and more.

How we test

30-point methodology with pro-grade equipment

Every product goes through the same rigorous process. We're talking weeks of real-world riding, not a quick spin around the block. GPS-verified range, real top speeds, hill climbs, brake distances. We collect all the data ourselves.

And we don't just test once and move on. We revisit products over time to track long-term reliability, and we update reviews as the market evolves and new competitors arrive.

See our full methodology
Editorial standards

Independent and transparent

We're reader-supported through affiliate links. When you buy through our site, we may earn a commission. It doesn't affect what you pay, and it's how we keep everything free.

Brands sometimes send us review units, but the data always leads. No pre-publish approval, no sponsored rankings. If a product underperforms, we say so.
